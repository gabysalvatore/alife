How and When was I used?

The fragments were once part of an ancient structure. Look at the image below. The stone for the Alife
arch might have been extracted from something similar.

Quick quiz: What type of ancient building do you think is shown here?

A) TOMB
Correct!

Grandiose ancient tombs in marble often included arcitectural elements like those on the Alife arch.

B) BASILICA
TRY AGAIN

The decorative pattern for the Alife arch was too small to adorn an ancient basilica. Basilicas were large scale public buildings which functioned as a location to carry out business and to engage in legal matters.

C) BATH
TRY AGAIN

The decorative pattern for the Alife arch was too small to adorn an ancient bath. Roman thermae were large scale centers used for bathing and for socializing.
