Where am I from?

The marbles from this arch were originally quarried in antiquity and re-used in the medieval period. Look at the map. Marble testing has helped us to identify the different ancient quarries.

Quick Quiz: How do you think the stone was primarily transported?

A) LAND
TRY AGAIN

Traveling by land was prohibitively expensive and time-consuming, particularly when transporting marble over mountainous terrain.

B) SEA
CORRECT!

The marble was mostly transported by sea using cargo ships. This was the most time-efficient and least expensive way to transport the stone.

C) AIR
TRY AGAIN

The marble from the arch was mined before air travel had been invented! The first successful airplane flight was several centuries later in 1903 by the Wright brothers.
